Service to humanity is service to God!

We at Khaan-दान aim to resolve the issue of excess food being wasted in hotels and restaurants in India on a large scale basis, as well as the issue of hunger and poverty that India currently faces. India currently ranks 105 out of 117 countries, below all South-East Asian countries on global hunger and poverty index. We at Khaan-दान hence aim to fix these issues by juxtaposing both the issues to compliment each other. We connect NGOs with hotels and restaurants so that the excess food generated daily by hotels and restaurants can be fed to the hungry people.
